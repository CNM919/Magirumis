About the Artist

Hi, I'm Alex! I'm 12 years old and I create crochet artwork that blends color, fun and the ultimate in cuteness — from small hanging ornaments to large cuddly animals, each piece is hand-crafted and one of a kind.

Every item is made by me inspired by designs from all around the world. I can't wait for you to enjoy a custom Magirumi!
